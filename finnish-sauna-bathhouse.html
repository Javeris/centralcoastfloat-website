Traditional Finnish Cedar Sauna

Step into our authentic Finnish cedar sauna. This stunning sauna delivers deep, consistent heat, creating a truly rejuvenating experience. Whether you're seeking stress relief, detoxification, or improved circulation, our sauna offers an immersive environment designed to help you unwind and restore balance.

Hot and Cold Mineral Cedar Spas – The Perfect Contrast Therapy

Our bathhouse features two round cedar mineral spas for hot and cold water immersion, offering a luxurious way to boost both physical and mental well-being.

  • Hot Mineral Spa: Soothe your muscles, improve blood flow, and relieve tension in our hot mineral spa. The warmth, combined with the therapeutic properties of minerals, helps detoxify your body, ease joint pain, and promote relaxation.
  • Cold Mineral Spa: Invigorate your senses and reduce inflammation in our cold mineral spa. Cold water immersion is known to enhance recovery, boost your immune system, and improve mental clarity. The added minerals provide further rejuvenation for the skin and body.
  • Contrast therapy: alternating between hot and cold immersion, stimulates circulation, accelerates muscle repair, and leaves you feeling refreshed and revitalised.

Communal Wellness in a Serene Bamboo Oasis

Our bathhouse is a communal space surrounded by the natural beauty of bamboo and golden cane palms. In addition to the sauna and spas, we provide changing rooms, hot and cold showers, and secure lockers for your convenience.

The communal nature of our bathhouse fosters a sense of belonging and connection. Sharing the experience with others enhances relaxation and elevates your mood, while also promoting mental well-being and social bonds. Whether you're coming solo or with friends, the sense of community is at the heart of our wellness philosophy.
