Our Story

From a Back Cabin to a Place People Call Home

Central Coast Float started with a first date — James took Amanda to a float, and she walked out knowing the Central Coast needed one. What began as a small setup run out of a back cabin at home nine years ago has grown into a full-spectrum natural wellness destination in Umina Beach.

We exist for the professional who can’t switch off, the tradie whose back never stops aching, the mum who puts everyone else first, and the teacher who’s forgotten what rest feels like.
