What Is the Bathhouse?

Cedar Heat. Cold Water. Complete Stillness.

Our bathhouse is a circuit of hot and cold — a Finnish cedar sauna, a hot magnesium mineral spa, and a cold plunge pool, all set in a tropical garden of bamboo and golden cane palms.

It's a communal space, calm and unhurried. If it's your first time, our staff will walk you through how it works. There's no right or wrong way — just move between hot and cold at your own pace.

The Finnish sauna sits around 80–90°C. The hot mineral spa is around 38–40°C. The cold plunge is around 8–12°C. You can move in and out at your own pace — there's no pressure to stay longer than feels right.
