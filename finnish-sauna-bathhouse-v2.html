About the Bathhouse

A Space Built for Slowing Down

Our cedar bathhouse sits in a tropical garden of bamboo and golden cane palms in Umina Beach. It's a communal space — meaning you might share the sauna with a stranger who becomes a regular face.

We built this place because the Central Coast didn't have one. Somewhere to actually switch off. No screens, no noise — just heat, cold water, and the sound of steam rising through the trees.

We provide changing rooms, hot and cold showers, lockers, filtered water, and herbal tea. You just show up.
